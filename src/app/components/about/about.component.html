MANUFACTURERS OF CORRUGATED BOXES

VSP TECHPACK, established in 2024 in Bangalore, is a trusted manufacturer of high-quality corrugated packaging solutions.

We specialize in delivering durable, cost-effective, and customized packaging for industries such as logistics, e-commerce, FMCG, and manufacturing.

With skilled manpower, modern machinery, and strong quality control, we ensure consistency, reliability, and timely delivery.
